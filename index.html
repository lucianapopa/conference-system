Executive Summary

A lightweight event system combining Eventbrite, Supabase, Metabase, and Chatbase to simplify registration and centralize all user and event data in one scalable structure.

Ticketing & Payments

📊

Metabase

Analytics Dashboard
  • Dashboard for analytics
  • Shows registrations and insights
💬

Chatbase

AI Chatbot Support
  • AI chatbot for FAQs (always available as a website pop-up)
  • Guides users to registration
03
How the data moves
Three simple paths keep every piece of information in the right place.
Registrations Eventbrite — sync → Supabase → Metabase Ticket sales flow back into our database and into the dashboard.
Questions 📝 Forms → Supabase Every question and interest signal is stored, never lost.
Chatbot 💬 Chatbot → Supports users & → Eventbrite Answers users and points them straight to registration.
